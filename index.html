About me

I graduated from UNC (Cordoba, Argentina) and then took postgraduate courses CBT and integrative psychotherapy. Once in London where I’m based, I’ve specialised in beheviour analysis and mental health I have skills to offer a more holistic experience to those who ask for help.

As a member of the British Psychological Society, I currently work online providing psychotherapeutic treatment to expats all over the world.

My motivation is to offer my full mind to those in need of speaking about their worries and personal crises. Because we’ve all been there some time, I know a helping hand can make a huge difference and prevent us from falling into darker moments.

Experience

With a wealth of experience as a psychologist, I have dedicated my professional practice in both clinical and educational environments for over 6 years. I have provided comprehensive support to people of diverse ages and diagnoses. My experience encompasses a range of evidence-based interventions, predominantly rooted in cognitive behavioural approaches and tailored to particular needs, sometimes including psychodynamic approaches to deliver integrated support. My commitment to the mental health field is reflected in my interests, including research and developmental initiatives to support neurodiversity and gender equality.
